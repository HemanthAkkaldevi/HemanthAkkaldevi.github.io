THE SECRET INGREDIENT IN MAKING PANIPURI NOBODY TOLD YOU ABOUT

  • Post On05.04.20
  • ByAkkaldevi Hemanth

Well, well, well...I will probably be showing this blog to my kids and..er.. their kids (I mean, why not) when I tell them about how I did not just wail away my time locked down while fighting off this invisible force- Corona. Anyway, welcome to my first blog post on my cooking feed. I'm happy you're here to read about how I made Pani Puri at home, and how I made it special.

I will try my best to explain the recipe in a simple way. The below recipe will serve 3-4 people. Follow the instruction and you should be good to go.

Ingredients :

For Puri :
  • 1 cup Ravva
  • 3 tablespoons maida
  • 1 pinch baking soda
  • 1/4th salt
For Stuffing :
  • Potatoes 4 to 5
  • White peas 1/2 cup
  • Finely chopped onion 1
  • Black salt 1/2 teaspoon (may vary according to taste)
  • Jeera powder 1 teaspoon
  • Coriander powder 1 teaspoon
  • Chat masala 1 teaspoon
For Pani :
  • A handful of coriander
  • A handful of mint leaves
  • 2 medium-sized green chili
  • 1-inch ginger
  • Tamarind water 1/2 cup
  • Salt to taste
  • Black salt 1 teaspoon (may vary according to taste)
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon lemon
  • 1 teaspoon chat masala
  • Water(3-4 glasses)

Step by step procedure for Puri :

  • step 1 - add all of the ingredients in a bowl and knead it to pizza dough consistency (sticky).
  • step 2 - put a wet cloth on top of that dough and leave it for 20 minutes.
  • step 3 - after 20 minutes knead the dough again and make thin roti's out of them partially see-through consistency.
  • step 4 - now using a cookie cutter or a cap cut the desired sizes of Puri.


  • step 5 - bring the oil to fuming hot level (smoking) and drop the puri's onto the oil and as soon as you drop the puri's, you need to press them using the spoon, puris won't puff if you don't press them and fry them till golden brown.
  • step 6 - do not eat them directly, make sure they're kept aside for 10 - 15 minutes so that the air will make them crisp.
  • that's it puris are done!!!!


Step by step procedure for Pani :

  • step 1 - take a hand full of coriander and a handful of mint leaves, one-inch ginger and 2 medium-sized green chilies into a mixer grinder.
  • step 2 - now add 1/2 cup of water and grind it to a smooth paste.


  • step 2 - now filter this paste with a cotton cloth into a bowl and add 3-4 glasses of water.
    (tip - do not throw away the filtered stuff, it'll be helpful somewhere else.)
  • step 3 - now add 1/2 cup tamarind water to it.
  • step 4 - add all the above-mentioned masala to the water and mix well.
  • step 5 - add masala's to adjust the taste, and add water if the masala is excess and re-adjust the taste.


Step by step procedure for Stuffing :

  • step 1 - soak the white peas in water for at least 4 hours.
  • step 2 - boil the white peas in a pressure cooker for about 5-6 whistles.
  • step 3 - take the potatoes and boil them in a pressure cooker for about 5-6 whistles.
  • step 4 - now peel off the skin of the potatoes and medium mash them.
  • step 5 - now add boiled white peas to the potatoes.
  • step 6 - remember the leftovers from the filtration of Pani? you can add it to the stuffing as well because it gives a nice flavor to it
  • step 7 - Now add onion all the above mentioned masala and mix them properly.
  • DONE!!!!


All the items are prepared and ready to be munched on!



That’s it for now guys, that’s how you prepare Pani Puri at home, I know the process looks lengthy and time taking but once you get hang of it, it will be a piece of cake for you guys to make. Thank you for reading please drop-in your valuable feedback on my Instagram post. adios!
