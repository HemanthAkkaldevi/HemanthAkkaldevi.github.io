HOW I MADE A LAPTOP STAND USING PVC PIPES

  • Post On20.06.20
  • ByAkkaldevi Hemanth

Ever since I got my new laptop I had plans to buy a laptop stand but then the pandemic happened. So then I thought, why not make one with the PVC pipes similar to the other projects I made using the stock PVC pipes that I have at home. Below is the tutorial for “How To Make A Laptop Stand”

Items required :

  • PVC pipes
  • 4 T- joints
  • 6 L- joints
  • Glue

Measurements :

Cut the pipe into the lengths mentioned below :
  • 2 pipes of 25cm
  • 2 pipes of 15cm
  • 4 pipes of 11cm
  • 2 pipes of 12cm
  • 2 pipes of 10cm

Use this image for reference :



Procedure:

  • 1) Connect one 15cm pipe and a 25cm pipe using an L-joint.
  • 2) Connect another 15cm and 25cm similarly, let’s call them “LEGS”



  • 3) Attach two 11cm pipes using a T- joint.
  • 4) Follow similar procedure with the remaining two 11cm pipes.
  • 5) Attach the two T- joints using a 10cm pipe, let’s call them “HANDS”



  • 6) now with the remaining pipes follow the order of 12cm -> T- joint -> 10cm -> T- joint -> 12cm which will make a single pipe lets call it the “BACKBONE”



  • 7) Follow the below image and connect the BACKBONE to the Legs using two L- joints.



  • 8) Once that is done, connect the HANDS to the BACKBONE T-joints using the reference image below.



  • 9) For extra protection attach two L- joints and small pieces of PVC pipe at the end of the HANDS so that the laptop won’t slip down.



And here is the final output :



The PVC pipes provide a strong structure which can hold the laptop. Choose the best PVC pipe that can withstand the weight of your laptop and make sure that you get the T- joints and L- joints that match the type of your PVC pipes.
